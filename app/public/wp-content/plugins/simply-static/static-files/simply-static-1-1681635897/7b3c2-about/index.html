About

Hi there! I’m Marcelo, a computer engineer who’s passionate about a lot of things, including biking, video games, and traveling. I currently live in Norway and work as a hardware engineer, but in my free time, you’ll often find me programming, tinkering with Raspberry Pi projects, and exploring the latest developments in cloud computing and Linux.

I’ve always been drawn to adventure, so whenever I get the chance, I love to pack my bags and explore new places and cultures. Whether it’s hopping on my bike to hit the trails or diving headfirst into a new video game, I’m always looking for new experiences to add to my ever-growing list.

Through my personal blog, I hope to share my engineering journey, document my travels, and connect with others who share my interests. So, join me on this adventure, and let’s explore the exciting world of engineering, technology, and travel together!

Marcelo C.F de Almeida
